Getting Started

What is Lord's Mobile?

Lord's Mobile is a massively multiplayer online real-time strategy game where you build your kingdom, train troops, form alliances, and battle players from around the world. With over 100 million downloads, it's one of the most popular mobile MMO strategy games available.

Starting Your Journey

When you first start playing Lord's Mobile, follow these essential steps to build a strong foundation:

  1. Complete the tutorial - It teaches the basic mechanics and rewards you with valuable resources
  2. Choose your starter kingdom carefully - Newer kingdoms are better for beginners as they have fewer established powerful players
  3. Select a good name for your castle - You can't change it later, so choose wisely
  4. Follow the quests - They provide valuable rewards and guidance for progression
  5. Join an active guild as soon as possible - Guilds offer protection, resources, and guidance

Construction Strategy

Upgrade your buildings strategically with these proven techniques:

  • Always have a building upgrading - use speed ups if available to maximize efficiency
  • Focus on your Keep first - higher level keeps unlock more features and buildings
  • Upgrade resource buildings evenly to maintain a balanced economy
  • Don't neglect your walls - they provide defensive bonuses and can deter attackers
  • Use the "Master's Blueprint" item for expensive building upgrades to save resources
  • Time your upgrades with "Mightiest Governor" events for additional rewards

Troops & Combat

Understanding Troop Types

Master the rock-paper-scissors combat system of Lord's Mobile with these troop types:

Infantry

Strong against cavalry, weak against ranged troops. Best for defending against cavalry attacks.

Cavalry

Strong against ranged, weak against infantry. Excellent for attacking ranged formations.

Ranged

Strong against infantry, weak against cavalry. Perfect for countering infantry armies.

Siege

Used for attacking castles, weak in direct combat. Essential for reducing castle durability.

Advanced Training Strategy

Follow these tips for training and maintaining a powerful army:

  • Focus on one troop type initially (recommend infantry or ranged for beginners)
  • Always keep your training queues active to continuously grow your army
  • Research military technologies to significantly improve troop stats and effectiveness
  • Use troop training speed boosts during events to maximize efficiency
  • Maintain a balanced army composition as you progress to handle different threats
  • Keep troops sheltered when offline to protect them from attacks
  • Use the infirmary to heal wounded troops instead of retraining new ones

Resource Management

Key Resources

Effectively manage these essential resources to maintain a thriving kingdom:

Resource How to Obtain Advanced Tips
Food Farms, hunting, quests, gathering Keep low stocks to avoid being raided. Use food boosts for training periods.
Wood Lumberyard, gathering, quests Always have production ongoing. Upgrade lumber mills during wood events.
Stone Quarry, gathering, quests Upgrade quarry early. Stone is crucial for building upgrades.

Advanced Resource Strategies

  • Upgrade your resource production buildings evenly to maintain balanced production
  • Use resource boosts during special events to maximize gathering efficiency
  • Gather from resource tiles on the world map regularly - higher level tiles yield more resources
  • Complete daily quests for resource rewards and event items
  • Join a guild with resource help benefits and resource exchange programs
  • Time your resource spending with events that offer rewards for spending resources
  • Use multiple resource alt accounts (farm accounts) to feed your main account

Pro Tips & Strategies

Essential Beginner to Advanced Tips

Join a Guild

Guilds provide protection, resources, and help with construction. Active guilds also offer valuable advice and support.

Use Peace Shield

Activate peace shields when offline for extended periods to protect your kingdom from attacks.

Complete Quests

Daily quests provide valuable rewards and experience. Event quests offer rare items and resources.

Save Gems

Don't waste gems on unnecessary items. Save for important upgrades like additional builders and research.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I spend gems on as a beginner?

As a beginner, focus on spending gems on these priorities: 1) Builder hut (second construction queue), 2) Important research that requires gems, 3) VIP level upgrades for permanent benefits, 4) Essential building upgrades during events. Avoid spending gems on speeding up construction or training unless during special events that offer additional rewards for doing so.

How can I protect my resources from attackers?

Use these strategies to protect your resources: 1) Use your vault to protect resources - upgrade it regularly, 2) Keep resource levels low by spending them on upgrades, 3) Use peace shields when offline for extended periods, 4) Join a strong guild for protection and shelter, 5) Shelter your troops when attacked to minimize losses, 6) Use the resource warehouse to store extra resources during events.

What is the best way to increase my power quickly?

To increase your power quickly: 1) Focus on building and research upgrades - they contribute significantly to might, 2) Train troops consistently - larger armies increase your might, 3) Complete all daily quests and events for rewards and boost items, 4) Join an active guild for help and bonuses, 5) Use speed ups strategically during boost events, 6) Focus on hero development and gear crafting for additional stats, 7) Participate in kingdom events for rare rewards and items.
